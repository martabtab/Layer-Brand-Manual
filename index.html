Concept

Shaping a more sustainable shopping through transparency.



Layer Technologies is a tech startup specialized in Blockchain. Its purpose is to connect sustainable brands to consumers. 

Brands can store sustainability certificates on Layer's blockchain-based database, and consumers will see them during their purchasing experience in a form of a widget, after installing the Layer browser extension. 

Having the knowledge at their fingertips, consumers will make more conscious purchasing decisions, ultimately resulting in a positive environmental impact and a shopping virtuous circle.


Layer is:

Transparent

Trustworthy

Conscious


Consumers which use Layer are:

Caring

Ethical


After using Layer, they will feel:

Empowered

Connected

Target: Consumer

Target

Layer's target is people aged 20-40 willing to lead a more sustainable lifestyle. They are willing to put time and effort to choose products from ethical brands. We want to give them the possibility to have real proof these brands are green not only with their words.


Friction

We address young people, busy on working or parenting. We must make good use of their little time. 


Tone of voice

The tone of voice will be fresh, straightforward and easy peasy


Follow the irrational.


The mood board is a powerful tool to unearth feelings and sensations about the brand.

It is made following unconsciously the flow and collecting images from an image dashboard. The images are then distributed on a canvas, always in a non-rational way.

We can now extract some visual elements which we will use to build the brand identity.

Typography helps the message come through.



Minimalist, contemporary and geometric. That's what the Raleway typeface's personality. 

It's a trustworthy, future-proof, very digital-looking sans serif. It can be found on Google Fonts on 9 weights.

Icons are visual 

guidance for words.



The icons used for the Layer identity should be resonating well with the Raleway typeface, keep an easy look and feel and be functional.


The Teenyicons free pack, containing 1160+ icons, keeps the tone fresh without renouncing to solidity.

We use colors to 

visually explain our values.



After creating mood board, I was quite surprised to see pink emerge. But definitely, it's a warm, proactive and fresh color. Light blue as the complementary one takes care of communicating trust, technology and youth.

Finally, white, with different shades, stands for transparency and ethics.
